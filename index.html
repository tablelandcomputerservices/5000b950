7. Condition of Service: Mandatory Data Backup

As a mandatory condition of this service, Tableland Computer Services & Repairs will perform a "Delta Image" or a "Full Forensic Backup" of the storage device intended for LMDE7 installation, prior to formatting of the storage device. This creates a backup of your current system that can be used at a later stage to revert your system back to its pre-installation state if required.

  • Delta Image Definition: A "Delta Image" is a snapshot of valid partitions only (standard system, recovery and data partitions with a valid partition signature) and explicitly excludes hidden partitions, hidden encrypted payloads, raw sector mappings, unused disk space and forensic metadata.
  • Full Forensic Backup Definition: A "Full Forensic Backup" is a full and complete sector by sector backup of an entire storage device, and includes hidden partitions, hidden encrypted payloads, raw sector mappings, unused disk space and forensic metadata.

As a mandatory condition of this service, you are required to inform us if you have any data that falls within the scope of a hidden partition, a hidden encrypted payload or a raw sector mapping.

Unless otherwise informed of such data, we will proceed to process a Delta Image before formatting the storage device. This will result in the permanent and unrecoverable loss of hidden partitions, hidden encrypted payloads, raw sector mappings, unused disk space and forensic metadata. In the event that you do inform us of any such data we will proceed to process a Full Forensic Backup instead.

To the extent permitted by law, and subject to your statutory guarantees under the Australian Consumer Law, Tableland Computer Services & Repairs accepts no liability for loss of data as a result of any failure by you to inform us of hidden data.

Your data and personal information will be securely stored using industry-standard encryption and will be handled in accordance with applicable Australian privacy laws.

Any backup of data we hold for you will be stored for an "active data retention period" that is concurrent to the support duration (2 calendar months) as defined in Section 4. Exactly 14 days after the active data retention period has concluded, any backup of data we hold for you will be automatically and securely erased, unless an extension is requested prior to the end of the active data retention period. You may request the deletion of your data at any time.
